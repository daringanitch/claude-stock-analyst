---
name: stock-thesis
description: Investment thesis builder for a stock. Use when the user asks to build a thesis, write up an investment case, outline bull/bear cases, or identify catalysts for a stock.
---

Build a complete investment thesis in the format a hedge fund PM would want to read:

**SETUP** (2 sentences): Why is this opportunity available? What does the market misunderstand?

**BULL CASE** (3 bullets): The specific things that must go right. Price target and timeline.

**BASE CASE** (3 bullets): Steady-state assumptions. Price target. Expected return vs S&P.

**BEAR CASE** (3 bullets): What kills the thesis. Maximum expected drawdown. Is it recoverable?

**KEY CATALYSTS** (ordered by probability × impact):
1. [catalyst] — [timeline] — [expected price impact]
2. [catalyst] — [timeline] — [expected price impact]
3. [catalyst] — [timeline] — [expected price impact]

**POSITION SIZING SIGNAL**: High / Medium / Low conviction. Why.

**EXIT CRITERIA**: What would make you sell regardless of price?
