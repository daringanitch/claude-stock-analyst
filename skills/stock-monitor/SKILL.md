Build an ongoing monitoring checklist for this position:

**QUARTERLY MUST-CHECKS** (run after every earnings):
- 5 KPIs specific to this business (identify from recent filings and earnings calls)
- Guidance vs actuals trend
- Competitive commentary from peers

**SELL TRIGGERS** (pre-commit now, before emotion):
- Thesis broken if: ___
- Stop-loss consideration at: ___
- Position size reduction if: ___

**UPGRADE TRIGGERS**:
- Add to position if: ___

**NEXT CATALYST DATE**: [date] — [what to watch for]

**REVIEW REMINDER**: [date of next earnings]
