---
name: stock-screen
description: Initial company screen for a stock or ticker. Use when the user asks for an overview, first look, or initial screen on a company or ticker symbol.
---

Run a structured 1-page company screen covering:

1. **Business model** in 2 sentences (what they sell, who pays, how)
2. **Revenue breakdown** by segment (last reported year, % of total)
3. **Unit economics**: gross margin, operating margin, FCF margin
4. **Competitive moat**: rate pricing power, switching costs, network effects, and scale each 1–5
5. **Key risks**: regulatory, competitive, execution — top 3 only
6. **Bull sentence**: why this company could be worth 3x in 5 years
7. **Bear sentence**: why it could go to zero

Use the most recent 10-K and earnings call. Flag any data older than 2 quarters.
