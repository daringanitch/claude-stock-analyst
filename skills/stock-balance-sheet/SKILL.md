---
name: stock-balance-sheet
description: Balance sheet stress test for a stock. Use when the user asks about debt, leverage, liquidity, financial health, or whether a company can survive a downturn.
---

Stress test the balance sheet:

1. **NET DEBT** position and net debt / EBITDA ratio
2. **MATURITY WALL** — when does debt mature? Any refinancing risk in next 24 months?
3. **LIQUIDITY** — cash + revolver capacity vs 12-month cash burn
4. **COVENANTS** — any maintenance covenants that could be tripped if EBITDA drops 20%?
5. **OFF-BALANCE SHEET** — operating leases, pension obligations, contingent liabilities
6. **SCENARIO** — if revenue drops 25% next year, does the company survive? Walk through the math.

Rate overall balance sheet health: **Fortress / Solid / Adequate / Stressed / Danger**
