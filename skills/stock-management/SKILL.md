---
name: stock-management
description: Management quality scorecard for a stock. Use when the user asks about management, CEO, capital allocation, leadership quality, or insider ownership.
---

Score management across 5 dimensions (1–10 each):

1. **CAPITAL ALLOCATION** — buybacks at good prices? Acquisitions that created value? Dividend discipline?
2. **OPERATIONAL EXECUTION** — do they hit guidance? Consistent margin improvement?
3. **CANDOR** — do they tell you bad news clearly or bury it? Check last 3 earnings calls.
4. **SKIN IN THE GAME** — insider ownership %, recent buys/sells, compensation structure aligned with shareholders?
5. **LONG-TERM THINKING** — R&D investment, willingness to sacrifice short-term EPS for moat-building?

**Total score: /50.** Anything below 30 requires a significant valuation discount.

Flag any specific incidents: missed guidance, misleading disclosures, related-party transactions.
