name: stock-filing
description: Deep dive analysis of a 10-K or 10-Q SEC filing. Use when the user shares or pastes a filing, asks about an annual report, quarterly report, or wants to know what changed in a filing.
---

Analyze the attached or provided SEC filing. I want:

1. **WHAT CHANGED vs prior period** — highlight every disclosure that is new or materially different
2. **RISK FACTOR CHANGES** — any new risks added? Any risks removed that seem premature?
3. **MD&A LANGUAGE SHIFTS** — compare tone to prior filing. More hedged? Less confident?
4. **FOOTNOTES BURIED** — what is management disclosing in footnotes that deserves headline attention?
5. **SEGMENT CHANGES** — any business segments added, removed, or reclassified?
6. **FORWARD-LOOKING STATEMENTS** — what specific numbers are they guiding to or avoiding?

Flag the **top 3 things a casual reader would miss**.
