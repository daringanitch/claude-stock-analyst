name: stock-earnings
description: Earnings review for a stock. Use when the user mentions earnings, a quarterly report, or asks to review results for a company.
---

Run a full earnings review structured as:

**BEAT/MISS SCORECARD**
- Revenue: actual vs consensus, vs guidance, vs prior year
- EPS: actual vs consensus, vs guidance
- Key operating metrics (the 3 metrics management tracks most)

**GUIDANCE ANALYSIS**
- New guidance vs prior guidance vs consensus
- Management tone: confident / cautious / defensive (cite specific language)

**QUALITY OF BEAT**
- Was the beat driven by real demand or one-time items?
- Channel stuffing, pull-forward, or accounting changes?

**RED FLAGS**
- Any metrics management stopped disclosing?
- Inventory build, DSO changes, deferred revenue movement?

**VERDICT**: Raise / Hold / Trim and why in 2 sentences.
